Space Pong

The newest extreme sport, Space Pong is the experience the Orbital Oasis is known for. People across earth come aboard to have a go at this game. Fitted with a jetpack, a rope, and a bat, two or more competitors fight to keep their ping pong ball from floating into deep space.

Space Walk

Want to go outside the space station, but aren't very competitive. Go on one of the space walks. This walk will get your blood pumping, and you can watch the space pong players from afar, no need for jetpack training either!
