Our Hotel History

The Beginning

The Orbital Oasis was built over two decades from a small station in orbit, to the largest structures in orbit today. The Orbital Oasis is built entirely in space, and orbits the earth 20 times per day.

Ralph Baer proposed the idea of simple games people could play on their own television sets in 1958. Atari founder Nolan Bushnell created pong. The company sold more than 8,000 pong arcade machines. Pong then got added to a console and became a favourite of many people at home. Though the popularity of pong declined in the 1980s due to more complex games being made - we're renewing this games popularity.

Current Time

Future

Olympic sport. Competitors will come from around the solar system to compete in the great sport of pong. Individual and team categories available - with support for the paralympics!
