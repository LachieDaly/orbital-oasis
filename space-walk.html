The Walk

Somehow, the space walk is the tamer of the two activities available at the oasis. Walk outside the resort in unprotected space. You'll be safely connected to a crane, and you can even watch other patrons playing Space Pong. If you're struggling with the extreme nature of pong, this is your first step - conquer your fear of space, and then you'll just be playing a regular game
