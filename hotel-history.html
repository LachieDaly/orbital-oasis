HOTEL HISTORY

The Beginning

The Orbital Oasis was built over two decades from a small station in orbit, to the largest structures in orbit today. In 1998 when the international space station was launched, we never expected it to be converted into a resort in orbit - let alone that it would be maintained for this amount of time. In face the international space station was about to be shutdown due to lack of funding. That all changed when the second space race began, and we wanted to secure our position in the space frontier.

Now

We're nearly ready for your arrival, we have had athletes test our space pong fields, engineers perform all the safety protocols required. Setup our teleporters and recyclable rockets. The Orbital Oasis is available to all across the globe.

Future

We hope to keep getting people to this incredible resort, and expand even further beyond. The orbital oasis was a huge accomplishment but we know that there is more we can do. You should expect improvements to the travel between earth and oasis, cost reductions. Televised space pong!
